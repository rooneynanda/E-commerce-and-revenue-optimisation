
# 📊 E-commerce Sales Analysis & Revenue Optimization

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to uncover **revenue drivers, customer behavior, and profitability trends**.  

The goal is not just to visualize data, but to **translate numbers into actionable business decisions** that can improve growth, retention, and profitability.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Track **monthly revenue trends** and identify growth patterns  
- Analyze **profitability across product categories**  
- Understand **customer contribution & segmentation**  
- Evaluate **payment behavior & order priority impact**  
- Identify **key business risks and opportunities**

## 🔍 Key Insights (Business-Focused)

### 📈 Revenue Trends & Growth
- Revenue shows **high volatility (±30%)**, indicating unstable demand  
- Strong growth months: **April (+37%), May (+38%)**  
- Major decline: **June (-22%), August (-18%)**  

👉 **Insight:** Business lacks demand consistency → requires better forecasting & retention strategies  

---

### 👗 Category Performance
- **Fashion** contributes ~55% of total revenue (~4.3M)  
- **Electronics** generates revenue but has **very low profit (~0.18M)**  

👉 **Insight:**  
- Double down on high-performing categories  
- Re-evaluate pricing/cost strategy in low-margin segments  

---

### 👥 Customer Behavior
- **~95% of revenue comes from Members (~7.5M)**  
- New & Guest customers contribute negligibly  

👉 **Insight:**  
Heavy dependency on existing users → **high business risk**  
👉 Strong need for **customer acquisition strategy**

---

### 💳 Payment Insights
- **Credit Card dominates (~5.8M revenue)**  

👉 **Insight:**  
Customers prefer convenience → opportunity for **cashback & card-based offers**

---

### ⚡ Order Priority Analysis
- “High” priority orders generate the most revenue  

👉 **Insight:**  
Urgency drives purchases → leverage with **flash sales & limited-time offers**

---

### 🛍 Product-Level Insights
Top-performing products include:
- T-Shirts  
- Watches  
- Running Shoes  
 **Insight:**  
Focus on **bundling, upselling, and inventory optimization** for fast-moving items  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- 📌 Improve **customer acquisition funnel** (reduce dependency on members)  
- 📌 Optimize **pricing strategy** in low-margin categories  
- 📌 Leverage **urgency-based marketing campaigns**  
- 📌 Invest more in **high-performing categories (Fashion)**  
- 📌 Use **data-driven demand forecasting** to reduce volatility
